What do these statuses mean?
Conditional Approved: Violations found, but facility can operate while correcting them. Must take corrective action.
Unsatisfactory Re-Inspection Required: Serious violations found. Facility can temporarily operate but must be re-inspected soon.
Closure Re-Inspection Required: Critical violations. Facility must close and cannot reopen until passing re-inspection.
CLOSED tag: Facility's operational status is currently closed (based on most recent inspection).
